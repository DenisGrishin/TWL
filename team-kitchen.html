“A freestyle kitchen…”

Stirring the senses, memories, emotions… Harmony is a skill. The humble mastery of simplifying the complex and infinite connections embodied in a dish. Building an ephemeral speech in a tangible form to awaken, through flavor, that which is humanly essential and endures in memory. Contrasts merged into a melody of acts and stories that narrate experiences lived from emotion, landscapes, products, magic and surprise. The fraternal interrelation of three worlds: solid, liquid and sweet—Joan, Josep and Jordi—to illustrate what we have eaten, what has moved us, what we have learned, where we are from, and where we are headed to: the world we dream of.

A kitchen connected to the earth as pantry and habitat, which dialogues with the small farmers who have grown with us. A kitchen that peeps into a world that wants to be human, genuine and sensitive.

Having all the techniques and innovations, in a continuous and conscious search for chimerical perfection in order to offer you a 360º experience, while remaining honored to have you visit us with an open heart. That moves us.

Welcome to a surprising kitchen without stridencies, where Joan’s mastery in the balance of opposites is rounded off by Jordi’s fresh creative anarchy, and the placid sound of Josep’s liquid tides of introspection, an aqueous universe that inspires, pairs and accompanies the interplay for the senses that is forged in this kitchen, in silence and tempering, for you.
